Buckhead Church Parking Info

Gate Code: 31265#

Parking Directions:

After turning from Lenox Rd, take an immediate right turn onto the road in front of the old Wells Fargo bank building & the Spring Hill Suites. You will be directly beside Buckhead Church. Follow this road around until you see a parking deck with a gate on your left. This gate should be open for morning sessions but should you find it closed the code above will open the gate. On the right side of the parking lot is a bank of elevators. Take an elevator to the first or second floor.



Buckhead Church 1st Floor Map

This is the ground floor and contains The Gallery, where food will be served, along with dining spaces, as well as the majority of the writing rooms.



Buckhead Church 2nd Floor Map

This floor contains the Theater, where group worship sessions will be held.
